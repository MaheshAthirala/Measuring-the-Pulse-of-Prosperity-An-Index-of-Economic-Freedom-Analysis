About the Project

Why Economic Freedom Matters?

Economic freedom plays a critical role in shaping prosperity, innovation, and living standards. This project analyzes economic freedom indicators across countries to understand their relationship with growth and development.

  • Global Comparison: Evaluate economic freedom scores across regions.
  • Policy Insights: Understand how regulatory efficiency and open markets influence growth.
  • Data-Driven Story: Discover patterns linking freedom, prosperity, and investment climate.
